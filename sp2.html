Clark City Marathon 2023

sports.png


Get ready for the most anticipated marathon event of the year, the CLARK CITY MARATHON 2023! The race course will leave you exhilarated and the atmosphere will truly inspire you. Run through the scenic Clark's New Sacobia bridge and experience the breathtaking 42.195 km Full Marathon Distance. We also offer a 21.095 km Half Marathon and shorter distances (10km, 5km) for all levels of runners! The official venue is Filinvest Mimosa+ Leisure City, Clark, Pampanga. To claim your Race kit, please bring your Govt-issued ID or Passport along with a copy of the registration confirmation email. If you are collecting the kit on behalf of someone else, please bring a copy of their Govt-issued ID or Passport and a copy of the registration confirmation email. TRAIN SAFELY and see you on RACE DAY, October 29, 2023!
