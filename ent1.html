NBC Inter-High set on October 13 in Pampanga

https://bit.ly/45vKpgk


The inaugural National Basketball Championships Inter-High, a nationwide search for the best private high school boys basketball team, unfolds on Friday, October 13 in Pampanga. Thirty-two provinces, cities, or regions will each host eight teams competing for the right to represent their province in the grand finals.

Presented and organized by Jams Sports, a division of Jamsap Entertainment Corporation, and with PBA legend and 2013 MVP Arwind Santos serving as league Commissioner, the 256-team win-or-go-home boys' tournament offers a grand prize of One Million Pesos (P1,000,000) for the champion. More than anything else, NBCIH will be a platform for 14 to 19-year-olds to showcase their basketball skills and potential in front of coaches and scouts, potentially leading to athletic scholarship offers from colleges and universities across the nation that are always on the lookout for fresh, young, and promising talent.
