Set for a Kapampangan food festival

October 10, 2023

SAN FERNANDO CITY -- The Pampanga Provincial Government will organize a week-long event emphasizing the province's rich culinary heritage and customs.

Second District Board -Member Mylyn Pineda Cayabyab initiated arrangements for the "Manyaman Food Festival."

According to the lady board member, Governor Dennis Pineda supports the event.

The event is scheduled to highlight Pampanga's rich culinary heritage, including heirloom recipes, cooking techniques, and food items.

Pineda-Cayabyab stated that the event will most likely coincide with the Pampanga Day celebrations in December.

The event will include cooking contests, cuisine exhibitions, and vendors showcasing Pampanga's several municipalities' products and food attractions.
