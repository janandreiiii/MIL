October 10, 2023

Pampanga Governor Dennis “Delta” Pineda has ordered the suspension of face-to-face classes on October 16-17 in the province due to a nationwide transport strike.

Executive Order No. 32-2023, which Pineda issued, covers all levels in public and private schools in Pampanga.

“In anticipation of the effects of the lack of public transportation to commuters especially to students and school personnel, it is deemed necessary to implement the suspension of face-to-face classes,” he said.

In order to avoid disruption in the academic calendar, Pineda urged the educational institutions to implement online, modular classes, or any alternative learning delivery modality applicable during those two days.

Transport group Malayang Alyansa ng Bus Employees at Laborers (MANIBELA) has announced its plan to conduct a nationwide transport strike starting October 16.

The group will protest the alleged corruption in the Land Transportation Franchising and Regulatory Board (LTFRB) and the government’s public utility modernization program.
